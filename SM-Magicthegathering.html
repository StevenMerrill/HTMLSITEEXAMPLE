Magic:the Gathering

Magic: the Gathering is trading card game that I play. I generally play Standard and Limited. Standard is a format that involves playing using a card pool of recently released cards. Limited involves opening packs and playing with the cards that you receive.

Preferred Colors

I don't have much of a preference of colors. I have a slight soft spot for red. However when playing limited I feel like I lean towards green.

To Brew or not to Brew?

When it comes down to deck brewing their are inherent risks to homebrew. Mainly since the Meta is so dominant it is hard to come up with strategies that compete. I am however a big fan of brewing and coming up with ideas that are different than the meta. I also have experienced success in my endeavors.

Limited Policy

I don't believe that their are any set ways to play limited, I do however believe that limited is quite like constructed in the idea that it is still completley necessary to have a plan. I also believe that limited might be helped by pulls, but the biggest factor in limited is how you build your deck.
